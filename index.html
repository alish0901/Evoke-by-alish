About Evoke Journey

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind your unforgettable journeys

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by passionate traveler Bibek Shrestha, Evoke Journey began as a small boutique travel agency with a big dream: to create meaningful, personalized travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist path.

What started as a one-person operation planning custom itineraries for friends and family has grown into a trusted travel service that has helped over 1,000 clients discover the world in extraordinary ways.

We believe travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create connections across cultures. This belief drives everything we do.

Our Mission

At Evoke Journey, our mission is simple but profound: to craft travel experiences that inspire, educate, and create lifelong memories while respecting the cultures and environments we visit.

Sustainable Travel

We're committed to responsible tourism that benefits local communities and preserves natural wonders.

Personalized Service

Every traveler is unique, and so is every itinerary we create.

Exceptional Quality

We partner with the best local guides and accommodations to ensure premium experiences.
